    What is homebrew?

    "homebrew" refers to characters, places, objects, etc. not made by Wizards of The Coast, however D&D is a lot about homebrew, but not recommended for new/noob players.

    Is D&D expensive?

    D&D will hurt your pocket if you don't have basic supplies, although this guide strives for cheap while not supporting piracy.

    What does D&D/DnD mean?

    It means Dungeons and Dragons.

    What is this guide for?

    This guide is for noobs who want a complete and up-to-date guide for D&D to be a starting point, OR a referance for veteran players.

    What do I need to know before starting?

    • You should be ready to learn and have some money & time.
    • This guide is kept up to date, but will stop supporting older versions of D&D when a new version comes out eg. 4th edition to 5th edition.
    • Roleplaying may seem awkward at first, but eventually you'll get used to it hopefully.
    • You'll need friends to play this game, it's not single player.
